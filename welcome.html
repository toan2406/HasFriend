How does HasFriend work?
If you are a facebook user

The first time you sign in our system with your facebook account, you will be given 500 coins for bet. If you refer one of your friend to a company, and he/she is accepted, you will receive the money equivalent to number of coins that you bet on him/her. Then, you can withdraw your money, or use it to buy more coins. If your friend fail, you will lose your coins. BE CAREFUL!
